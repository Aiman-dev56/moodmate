🧠 Mental Health Tips

🌞
Start Your Day with Gratitude

Write down 3 things you’re thankful for each morning. It boosts positivity.

🧘
Practice Mindfulness

Spend 5-10 minutes daily focusing on your breath and letting go of tension.

📵
Limit Screen Time

Take digital breaks and avoid screens at least 30 minutes before bed.

💬
Talk It Out

Don't bottle it up — talk to a friend, therapist, or write it in a journal.

🚶‍♂️
Take Walks

A 15-minute walk in nature can calm your mind and improve your mood.

😴
Sleep Well

Create a bedtime routine and aim for 7–8 hours of restful sleep.
